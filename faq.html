Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and policies

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment through our website using the online booking form, call us at (555) 123-4567, or visit us in person. We recommend booking in advance, especially for weekend appointments and special services.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 1-2 weeks in advance for regular services and 3-4 weeks for special occasions like bridal services or holiday appointments. Same-day appointments may be available based on availability.

Can I request a specific stylist or therapist?

Absolutely! You can request your preferred stylist or therapist when booking. Please note that specific requests may affect availability, so we recommend booking further in advance.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 24-hour notice for cancellations. Cancellations made less than 24 hours in advance may be subject to a 50% service charge. No-shows will be charged the full service amount.

What services do you offer?

We offer a full range of spa services (massages, facials, body treatments), barber services (haircuts, shaves, beard grooming), and salon services (hair styling, coloring, treatments, extensions). View our complete service menu on our Services page.

Do you offer package deals or memberships?

Yes! We offer several package deals and a VIP membership program with exclusive discounts and priority booking. Check our Pricing page for current packages and membership benefits.

How long do different services take?

Service times vary: Haircuts (45-60 min), Massages (60-90 min), Facials (60-75 min), Beard grooming (30-45 min), Hair coloring (2-3 hours). Exact timing will be confirmed when booking.

Do you use organic or natural products?

We use premium, professional-grade products including many organic and natural options. Our product lines include Aveda, Olaplex, and other high-quality brands. We can accommodate specific product preferences when possible.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), and digital payments (Apple Pay, Google Pay). Gift cards are also available for purchase.

What is your tipping policy?

Gratuity is not included in service prices and is at your discretion. Standard tipping is 18-20% for excellent service. Tips can be added to credit card payments or given in cash.

Do you have age restrictions?

Children under 16 must be accompanied by a parent or guardian. Some spa services have age restrictions (18+ for certain treatments). We offer family-friendly services and can accommodate children's needs.

What should I wear to my appointment?

Wear comfortable clothing. For spa services, we provide robes and disposable undergarments. For hair services, wear something with a loose neckline. We'll provide protective capes and towels as needed.

What health and safety measures do you have in place?

We maintain the highest hygiene standards with sanitized equipment, disposable items where appropriate, regular deep cleaning, and health screening protocols. All staff are trained in current health and safety guidelines.

Do I need to wear a mask?

Mask requirements follow current local health guidelines. We respect personal preferences and can accommodate clients who prefer to wear masks. Our staff will follow the same protocols you're comfortable with.

What if I'm feeling unwell?

Please reschedule if you're feeling unwell or have any symptoms. We'll waive cancellation fees for health-related cancellations. Your health and the health of our staff and other clients is our priority.

Do you offer bridal and event services?

Yes! We specialize in bridal and special event styling. We offer on-site services, trial runs, and group packages. Book a consultation to discuss your special day needs and create a customized package.

Can I purchase gift cards?

Gift cards are available in any denomination and can be purchased online, by phone, or in person. They make perfect gifts and never expire. Digital gift cards can be emailed directly to recipients.

Do you have parking available?

Yes, we have complimentary parking available for all clients. Our parking lot is well-lit and secure. Valet parking is available for special events and VIP members.

What if I'm not satisfied with my service?

Your satisfaction is guaranteed. If you're not completely happy with your service, please let us know within 48 hours. We'll work with you to make it right, whether that's a complimentary touch-up or full service redo.
